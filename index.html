About

Hi, this is Raunak Mishra, a higher secondary graduate from St. Xavier's College, Maitighar, Kathmandu, Nepal currently pursuing my 2nd semester of Undergraduate in Electrical Engineering at IoE - Pulchowk Campus, Tribhuvan University, Nepal. I'm very fond of Maths and Computers. I'm a 20-year-old guy. The thing of which, I'm very fond of are: Physics, Computers, Gadgets and recent technological advancements. Also, my favorite games are cricket and football for outdoor and ludo, chess and carrom for indoor.
Some of my works like "COVID-19 Spread Breaking Device for Public Places" make me feel proud of myself. Altough I haven't experienced life much, I try my best to know more and more about life. I always enjoy my own company. On this website I just share some of my experiences, works, and projects. Furthermore, making everyone able to directly connect with me.
